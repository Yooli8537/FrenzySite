Champions

Myth: Spirit Sailor

The Spirit Sailor is Frenzy's first Mythic Tier Champion. He is a Melee only Character focused on movement and close range combat.


Abilities


Passive Abilities

Name Effect
Spiritual regeneration 50% less regeneration but starts 3s after leaving combat.
Soul Leap Sliding charges your next jump up, allowing you to jump much further and faster.
Double Jumps 3 double jumps

Active Abilities

Soul consumption Consume a killed enemy's soul in order to heal yourself.
Default Keybind F
Duration 1.5s + 0.5s Charge
Healing 75 + Invincibility
Realm Shift Dash 7m in the direction you're looking by switching into the second realm, including through walls, becoming invincible for the dash's duration. After you exit the second realm, you gain a 20% speed boost for 3s.
Default Keybind E
Duration 0.2s + 0.2s Charge
Max Stack 2
Cooldown 12s
Tortured Souls Dealing damage and using abilities charges up a spirit meter. Once this is full, you can summon a horde of 12 spirits to fight alongside you. They form a kind of protective barrier around you and pounce enemies that dare to come too close. The Spirit Sailor also gets a 30% speed increase for 20s after activating this skill. Each spirit has 75hp and deals 40 damage / second. Each bullet the spirits absorb also increases the spirit meter slightly.
Default Keybind Q
Points required 5'000
Meter fill on Damage 1
Meter fill on Soul Leap Slide duration (s) * 3
Meter fill on Double Jump 2
