Champions

Myth: Spirit Sailor

A vengeful soul forever bound to the tides between realms. The Spirit Sailor is Frenzy's first Mythic Tier Champion. He is a Melee only Character focused on movement and close range combat with no Utility.
Mission Cost: 1'500'000 coins
