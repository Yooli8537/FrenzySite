Champions

In Frenzy, you can choose between a wide roster of champions. Each of them provide you with unique abilities that you can use to give yourself an advantage in combat.

Common Champions

Common champions are cheap champions best suited for beginners. They cost between 2'000 and 10'000 coins.

Rare Champions

Rare champions are a good middleground between the common and epic champions. Their abilities are more advanced whilst still remaining easy to understand. They cost between 10'000 and 30'000 coins.

Epic Champions

Epic are the most advanced champions that can be unlocked from the shop, costing between 30'000 and 100'000 coins. Their abilities lean more into understanding the game's and champion's mechanics in order to gain a bigger advantage.

Legends

Legends are Champions who've transcended limits. Through sheer mastery and obsession, they've learned to control the chaos of battle and turned it into an art form. Every movement, every shot, every kill, it carves their name deeper into Frenzy's history.

Legends can only be unlocked by completing special missions costing between 200'000 and 600'000 coins.

Beyond forgotten

Little more than myths remain of these people. Their lives shrouded in mystery and uncertainty, some of them thought to not even exist at all, until they are proven otherwise. Once legends, these myths don't want glory, they don't want to master the battlefield - they just want to get back what was taken from them.
