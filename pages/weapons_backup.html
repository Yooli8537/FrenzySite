Weapons

Frenzy has a lot of weapons to offer in order for you to try and find what weapons work best with what characters. Since the weapons page in-game shouldn't be too overwhelming, some less important stats and additional info isn't shown. This is why this page exists. Since there's more room to work with, players can more easily view all of the info they want on whatever weapon they want.

Primary Weapons: Assault Rifles

Assault rifles are the default weapon class. Every champion excluding legends has access to assault rifles. They are a reliable option that you can always fall back to in a pinch.

Primary Weapons: Burst Rifles

Burst rifles are similar to assault rifles, but they only shoot a few bullets at a time, go on cooldown and then shoot again a second or so later. They are basically a direct counterpart to the assault rifle with a very similar yet very distinct playstyle.
