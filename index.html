Frenzy

Frenzy is a chaotic and fast-paced first-person shooter hosted on Roblox that challenges players to master both movement and tactical gameplay. Each match offers new possibiliets to unlock your Frenzy, empowering you with godlike powers. With a variety of characters, weapons, and maps, Frenzy offers a dynamic experience that rewards skill, creativity, and clever use of your environment.
On Frenzy's official website, you can explore detailed information about the game's characters, their unique abilities, and specialized weapons. Each map is carefully designed to provide opportunities for both aggressive and strategic playstyles, ensuring that every match feels fresh and engaging.
